The goal here is **build something usable in 3 weeks**, not the full 10-week system.

# 3-Week MVP Development Plan for ToolRent SaaS

Goal of MVP:

```
Rental business signs up
→ Adds tools
→ Customer browses tools
→ Customer books tool
→ Customer pays deposit + rent
→ Admin manages booking
```

That is enough to validate the product.
