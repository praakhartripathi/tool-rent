Your plan is **B2B SaaS for rental shops**, which is great. But you can add **features that create a strong competitive advantage**.

## Add Delivery / Logistics Module

Right now your flow assumes **pickup**.

In India many rentals require **delivery**.

```

- Third-party courier
```
